How To Play Tic-Tac-Toe

This online Tic-Tac-Toe game connects players through Node.js and Socket.IO. Follow the steps below to learn how to play.

1. Getting Started

  • Enter a unique screen name and click Submit.
  • If the name is taken, you will be asked to choose another one.
  • After logging in, you will see all players and their statuses.

2. Player Status

  • Idle: Not playing or waiting.
  • Waiting: Created a game and waiting for an opponent.
  • Playing: Currently in a match.

3. Starting or Joining a Game

  • Click NEW GAME to start and choose X or O.
  • Other players can click JOIN to play against you.
  • When both players are ready, the board appears.

4. Game Rules

  • The board is 3 by 3. X always goes first.
  • Players alternate turns, clicking cells to place their symbol.
  • Three symbols in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.
  • If no moves remain and nobody wins, it is a draw.
